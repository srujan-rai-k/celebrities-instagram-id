12.The physical layer of HART operates in which of the following frequency band?

a) 800-900 MHz
b) 2.4 GHz
c) 800 MHz
d) 900 MHz

Answer: b
Explanation: The HART Physical Layer is based on the Bell 202 standard, using frequency shift keying (FSK) to communicate at 1200 bps. The signal frequencies representing bit values of 0 and 1 are 2200 and 1200Hz respectively.
