Breaking News: Plane Crash in Kerala 16 dead and 123 injured

Kerala: Its been reported that out of 184 passengers who were on board nearly 16 people were dead including pilot.

Rescue operations are under way,with Emergency Services are at the place.The incident happened around 7pm(IST) today.
